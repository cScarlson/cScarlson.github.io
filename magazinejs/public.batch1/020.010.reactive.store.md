
Reactive Store
================

## Myth
_You need a library like Redux or similar to have a Reactive Store_.

## Reason
A Reactive Store can be accomplished in roughly 25 LOC using a `Subject` from _The Observer Pattern_ (see previous chapter).

## Synopsis
By creating a store in about 15 LOC (Lines Of Code) that extends a `Subject`, and leveraging a Reducer that is about 5 LOC, we accomplished a Reactive State Store that is reusable across any domain. In fact, we even leverage the Reducer's `execute` method to override it for specific needs. For now, we spare you from some of the implementation details which we describe intimately in the next article that exists as a formal, Gang of Four style Software Design Pattern specification
