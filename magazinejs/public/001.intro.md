
Demystifying Frontend Toolchains and Industry Myths
================================================================

In the following articles & sections, we take a look at ways we tend to get hoodwinked by authors of things as small as plugins or libraries & frameworks and as large as Code Bootcamps and the AI industry. We loosely organize these into the following parts.

- Frontend Toolchains & patterns.
- Syntax, Linting, Formatting, Standards & "Definsive Coding Practices"
- Related Industries
- A Special Treat

While the first part's fun might outway any frustrations you may experience as you realize someone pulled the wool over your eyes, the following sections thereafter are not for the faint of heart. We take no prisoners in our opinions over the course of those sections. In fact, **this is a fair warning**: this book is going to make you angry, if we do our job right. That said, please take this collection of articles for what they are worth to you, think critically, and take what is of value from it to yourself.
